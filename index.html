Our Solar System

Planets from Closest to Farthest from the Sun

Mercury

The smallest planet, closest to the Sun with extreme temperature variations.

Venus

Earth's "sister planet" with a thick, toxic atmosphere and scorching temperatures.

Earth

Our home planet, the only known world with life and liquid water on its surface.

Mars

The Red Planet! Home to the tallest mountain in our solar system.

Jupiter

The biggest planet with a giant storm that's been raging for centuries!

Saturn

The planet with beautiful rings made of ice and rock!

Uranus

An ice giant that rotates on its side, with faint rings and many moons.

Neptune

The windiest planet, with speeds up to 1,200 mph in its blue atmosphere.
